About Gojo Saturo


Satoru Gojo is a fictional character in the popular Japanese manga and anime series, Jujutsu Kaisen. He is one of the most powerful jujutsu sorcerers in the series and serves as a mentor and father figure to the protagonist, Yuji Itadori.
